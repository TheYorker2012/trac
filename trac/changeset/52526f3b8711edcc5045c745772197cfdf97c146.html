     25 *      - '/carding'          (card requested)

     28 *      - '/user/$entity_id'  (specific user) 
     29 *      - '/card/$card_id'    (user with specific card) 
     30 *      - '/search'           (match posted search) 
     31 * 
     32 * Each filter option without '/not' is added as a disjunct. 
     33 * Each filter option which has '/not' is added as a conjunct on top of the 
     34 *      disjunction of '/not'less filter options. 
     35 * 
     36 * e.g. to get users in tech and news team who haven't submitted cards but have 
     37 *      paid the membership fee:
